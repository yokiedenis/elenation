About Us

Founding Story

It was founded in 2023 by a group of energetic youth activists, who came together with the objective of improving and upgrading lives of young girls in Ugandan communities.

They also realized the necessity of this establishment as a means of contributing to development and meeting communities' needs which has been the driving force and motivation.

We aim at achieving sustainable results from developmental activities and initiatives to our communities, especially with the youth, young girls and single mothers

Core Values

  1. Believe in the power of committed youth individuals and organizations that contribute to a sustainable development
  2. Working with efficiency, efficacy and long term sustainability.
  3. We believe in committed, selfless, godly and service oriented individuals who want to make a positive impact to their communities.
  4. We value the principle of social justice, and empowerment of youth, and women.
